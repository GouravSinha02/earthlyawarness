Air pollution is the presence of harmful or undesirable substances in the Earth's atmosphere, which can have detrimental effects on human health, the environment, and the climate. Water pollution refers to the contamination of water bodies such as lakes, rivers, oceans, groundwater, and even drinking water sources with harmful substances, making the water unsafe or unsuitable for its intended use.
